About Us

Sinarqya Food Court, established in 2022, stands as a vibrant culinary hub that has quickly become a beloved destination for food enthusiasts and casual diners alike. Located in the heart of the city, its architecture blends modern design with elements that reflect the cultural heritage of the region, creating an inviting atmosphere from the moment you step inside.

Our mission is to provide a diverse range of high-quality food options in a comfortable and welcoming environment. We are committed to supporting local businesses and fostering a sense of community through our culinary offerings.
